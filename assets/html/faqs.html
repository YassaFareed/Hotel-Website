FAQs

Q. How do I make a Booking?

A. It is really easy! You just need to go to Booking which is present on top of the page, after clicking on it you need to enter your information and payment method and click on "BOOK NOW" to book. You would be notified about your successful booking the instance you click on "BOOK NOW"


Q. We have two small children; can we get extra beds in the room?

A. Generally one extra bed for one child is available with some minor addition in charges.


Q. How can I view pictures of hotel?

A. It is quite simple! You just need to go to gallery which is present on top of the page. There you can see our awesome collection of images.


Q. Do I pay a reservation fee?

A.No, we will not charge you a reservation fee.


Q. My credit card has been charged. How can I get a refund?

A.We don't charge credit card with confirmation 2 times, if you confirmed two times you need to contact by calling us on hotel's number


Q. How can I request an early check-in or late check-out time?

A.We advise you to contact hotel by calling on our number for details.


Q. I do not have a credit card. Can I still book a room?

A. We are really sorry but it's hotel's policy to book using credit card to guarantee a reservation.
